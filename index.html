## Why ActivityPub? ActivityPub and the fediverse have brought answers to some important issues with social media platforms.
Instead of a top-down “product design” approach to building an app or platform, we want a diverse DIY ecosystem to meet the unique needs of different users and communities
Every user, community, developer, designer, project, and app has things to contribute to the common pool of knowledge, tools. We're trying to build a toolkit that helps piece all of those together into custom experiences dependending on each's need.

# Bonfire
### Bonfire will be... #### 1. a device #### 2. a fediverse hosting service #### 3. a CommonsPub app
### 1. Bonfire the device - use CommonsPub apps offline or with people on your local network (Temporary Autonomous Zones) - ready to use (plug and play) - based on Rasperry Pi
### 2. Bonfire the fediverse hosting service - syncs between your Bonfire and the fediverse when is online - link it to your own domain name (or use `your-instance.bonfire.cafe`) - runs open source software you can self-host, and others could run compatible services
### 3. Bonfire the CommonsPub app - shows all your fediverse feeds and notifications in a single place - end-to-end encryption (keys on the device) - run it on a Bonfire device, or on your own computer
Bonfire will build upon the CommonsPub ecosystem as an attempt to ensure sustainable FLOSS developement and maintainance. Crowdfunding campaign to help make it happen.
**Stay tuned at [bonfire.cafe](http://bonfire.cafe)**
